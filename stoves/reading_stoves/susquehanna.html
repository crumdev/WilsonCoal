The Susquehanna is ideal for large homes, garages and workshops. This furnace is designed to be connected directly into duct work. The large hopper and ash pan will allow you to set the unit to burn up to 3 days, without the worry of refueling. The patented Tri-Burner efficiency stoker system is easy to use, reliable, and constructed to exacting standards and is fully automatic.
